COMPANY PROFILE

Isenghard Limited is a specialized mechanical and engineering services company based in [Ntotroso, Asutifi North District, Ahafo Region Ghana]. We offer a comprehensive range of services including mechanical and hydraulic transmission repairs, welding and fabrication, structural engineering projects, and services for heavy-duty vehicles and equipment. Our mission is to provide high-quality, reliable, and cost-effective solutions to meet the needs of our clients in various industries.

Isenghard Limited was founded in [Year 2017] by [Founder's Name Alfred Nassarah] with the vision of becoming a leading provider of specialized mechanical and engineering services in the region. Our company operates from a modern facility equipped with state-of-the-art tools and machinery to ensure efficient and high-quality services. We are committed to delivering exceptional customer service and building long-term relationships with our clients.

BUSINESS OBJECTIVES

The objectives for Isenghard Limited 

  • To create a service-based company whose goal is to exceed customer's expectations and becomes a return client.
  • To increase the number of clients services by at least 10% per year through superior performance and word-of mouth referrals.
  • Have a clientele return rate of 90% by end of first year.
  • Become an established community destination by end of first year.
  • To hire local employees to help stimulate the economy.
  • To bring back the community's trust in the auto repair industry.
  • To provide excellent service for domestic and foreign automobiles and mining equipment

OUR CORE VALUES

  1. Integrity - Our business conduct includes the highest standards of honesty, ethics and virtue. We strive not to endanger employees, clients or our company
  2. Quality – We ensure that projects are finished within the guidelines established by the Scope of Work
  3. Reliability – Customers can also rely on us as we always deliver our services on time
  4. Teamwork – We strive to achieve our goals by combining our skills and knowledge and sharing our responsibilities as a team.
  5. Safety and environment - Our safety and that of our employees are very important to us. We also ensure that our activities do not harm the environment and take measures to ensure a sustainable environment.

Services Offered:

At Isenghard Limited, we offer a wide range of services to meet the diverse needs of our clients:

  • Mechanical and hydraulic transmission repairs for vehicles and equipment.
  • Welding and fabrication services for structural components and machinery.
  • Structural engineering projects including design, analysis, and construction.
  • Maintenance and repair services for heavy-duty vehicles and equipment such as trucks, cranes, and agricultural machinery.
