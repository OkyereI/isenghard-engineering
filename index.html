    About Us

    The Company offers general mechanical repair services, welding & fabrication, electrically both auto and power not limited to other mechanical and structural engineering works

    Isenghard Company Limited offers a wide range of services. It is ultimately the goal of the company to offer a one-stop facility for all mechanical/hydraulic/auto-servicing needs, including brakes, manual and auto transmission both light and heavy duty, wheel alignment, welding/fabrication etc. In this way, the company can offer greater perceived value for the customer than many other shops, which specialize in certain areas. The industry is highly competitive with suppliers having a great deal of power in setting and negotiating the prices of their products and services to repair shops. In addition, because the customers/clients see the service as undifferentiated and a "commodity" with little value separation between competitors, buyer power is also very high. Finally, the barriers to entry are moderately low, and the large number of competitors in this field, including substitutes (such as do-it-yourself work) means that the pricing for such services are very competitive. The only way to have an advantage in this industry is a low-cost leadership principal applied aggressively or to create higher switching costs through the building of strong business to customer ties. Isenghard Limited will hire trained and certified mechanics, welders, fabricators, electricians, and many others that are able to prove they have superior customer awareness and interaction. It is the company's professional people who will fulfill the firm's contracts and goals. The largest part of the company's expenses will be in labor costs. 4.0 Market Analysis Summary Since Isenghard Limited is able to service any vehicle on the road, equipment of all kinds including motorcycles and whatever the travelling public uses, it does not make any sense to segment our market. Our potential customer includes every household/contractor/road user in the district/region that owns one or more equipment/vehicles including welding and fabrication/HVAC. The industry does not have any seasonality that affects it.
